Biography

Bob is a retired engineer. He became interested in nature and in wildflowers in the 1970s and 80s when he took countless backpacking trips to the Sierra Nevada, Los Padres National Forest, and the Grand Canyon. His interests in photography date further back to the day his father presented him with a simple camera when he was in high school.
